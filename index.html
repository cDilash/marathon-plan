Zone Purpose Guide

Zone 2 — Easy / Aerobic Base

The backbone. Conversational pace builds capillaries, mitochondria, and fat-burning. 80% of your running lives here.

Zone 3 — Tempo / Threshold

"Comfortably hard." Builds lactate threshold. One tempo/week can raise threshold ~10%. Marathon pace lives here.

Zone 4–5 — Intervals / VO2max

Short, sharp repeats (400m–1200m) for speed and aerobic power. One session/week in Build/Peak only.

07
Adaptive Rules

Train Smart,
Not Just Hard

HRV-Guided Intensity

Check readiness daily. Hard workouts on high-HRV days. If HRV drops for 2–3 days → easy only.

Low HRV 2+ days → skip intensity, Zone 2 only

Missed Workout Policy

Never "make up" missed sessions. Continue as scheduled. Drop easy runs before long runs.

When in doubt, do less

Deload Every 3–4 Weeks

Cut ~20–30%. Shorter runs, skip hard session. This is where fitness actually consolidates.

Deload weeks = adaptation weeks

ACWR Sweet Spot

Keep ratio 0.8–1.3. No jumps over 10%/week. Injury risk spikes with load spikes.

ACWR ~0.9 = fitness > fatigue
